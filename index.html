CASA TOSCA JAVEA

Welcome to Casa Tosca, your enchanting refuge nestled in the lively heart of historic Javea. A 200 year old restaured house with 4 apartments and 1 ensuite room.

Our Location

For your exploration of the Costa Blanca, our location in Javea is perfect. Nestled in the charming old village, we are conveniently close to an array of fine restaurants and shops to cater to all your needs. Just 2 km away from the picturesque pebble beach, our place offers the ideal blend of convenience and scenic beauty. Centrally positioned between two airports, Valencia and Alicante, we ensure easy accessibility.
