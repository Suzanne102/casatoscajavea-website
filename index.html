Welcome to Casa Tosca, your enchanting refuge nestled in the lively heart of historic Javea. A 200 year old restaured house with 4 apartments and 1 ensuite room.

Our Location

Located right in the center of town, we are in between Alicante and Valencia Airports. The bus station in Javea is walking distance to the apartments.
