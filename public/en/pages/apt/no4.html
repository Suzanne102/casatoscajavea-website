Enjoy the tranquility of the Attic Apartment, a spacious hideaway full of Old Town charm. Simply furnished, it offers a warm and functional atmosphere. In addition to the main bed, a queen bed on the mezzanine can comfortably accommodate one or two extra guests — though the staircase to the loft is not suitable for small children. With approximately 80 m² (900 sq ft), the apartment is well equipped for cooking, including an oven, microwave, toaster, coffee maker, and—rare in the Old Town—a dishwasher. Towels and linens are provided, and a washing machine is available. In winter, the small fireplace adds a warm, cozy touch. The apartment is also located next to the shared terrace, perfect for enjoying the outdoors. Throughout your stay, you’ll have fast, reliable fibre-optic interne.
