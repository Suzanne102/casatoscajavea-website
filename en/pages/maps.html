How to Get to Casa Tosca

✈️ By Air:

Casa Tosca is located halfway between two major international airports: Valencia (VLC) and Alicante (ALC). Both are approximately 1.5 hours by car from Jávea.

🚄 By Train: From Madrid or Barcelona

There is now a fast AVE train from Madrid and Barcelona to Valencia, taking just about 2 hours.

🚕 By Car or bus:

From Valencia or Alicante airports/train stations to Jávea:

  • Taxi: approx. €120-150, duration around 1h20-1h30min. Alicante is a little closer..
  • Bus: 2.5–3 hours. Check options with ALSA (Around 15Euros). The Bus station in Javea is walking distance from Casa Tosca.
  • Blabla Cars can be a good option too.

🚗 Parking in Jávea Old Town

Public parking is available nearby:

  • Parking de la Constitución
  • Parking del Clot

📍 How to Reach Casa Tosca

We are located in the heart of Jávea’s historic Old Town:

Carrer En Forn 2, 03730 Jávea, Alicante

You can walk from both parking areas in under 5 minutes. You can drop your luggage on Virgen del Pilar but you cannot park there.

🚴 Exploring Jávea

We are just 2 km from the Port beach and 4 km from Playa del Arenal — perfect for a scenic bike ride. We offer bicycle rentals for €10/day.

🏡 Services in the Village
