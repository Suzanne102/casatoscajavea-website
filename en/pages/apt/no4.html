Apartment No.4 The Attic – 60 m²

Apartment No.4 “The Attic” is a spacious top-floor apartment in the heart of Jávea Old Town, offering rustic charm with exposed beams and Tosca stone details. Ideal for families or small groups, it combines generous space with a warm and authentic atmosphere.

The apartment features two queen beds, a fully equipped kitchen, air conditioning, heating, Smart TV, Wi-Fi, and direct access to the shared terrace.

Enjoy the tranquility of the Attic Apartment, a spacious hideaway full of Old Town charm. Simply furnished, it offers a warm and functional atmosphere. In addition to the main bed, a queen bed on the mezzanine can comfortably accommodate one or two extra guests — though the staircase to the loft is not suitable for small children.

With approximately 80 m² (900 sq ft), the apartment is well equipped for cooking, including an oven, microwave, toaster, coffee maker, and—rare in the Old Town—a dishwasher. Towels and linens are provided, and a washing machine is available. In winter, the small fireplace adds a warm, cozy touch. The apartment is also located next to the shared terrace, perfect for enjoying the outdoors. Throughout your stay, you’ll have fast, reliable fibre-optic internet.
