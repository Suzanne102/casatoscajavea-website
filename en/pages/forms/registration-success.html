Entry Code & Key Instructions

  • Enter the code which will be given to you prior to your arrival and turn the dial to the right to unlock the door.
  • Inside, walk past 2 steps before the main staircase.
  • On the right wall, you'll find a key box with apartment keys labeled 1–4.
  • Please return your key to the box before you leave.
